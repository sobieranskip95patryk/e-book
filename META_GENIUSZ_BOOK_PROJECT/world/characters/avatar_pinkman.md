## üéØ RULES FOR COPILOT WRITING PINKMAN

### **DO:**
- Show internal complexity and contradiction
- Include multiple levels of awareness simultaneously  
- Demonstrate growth through dialogue and action
- Show vulnerability alongside power
- Include philosophical depth in casual conversations
- Show care for others influencing decisions

### **DON'T:**
- Make him all-knowing or perfect
- Resolve philosophical tensions too easily
- Use him as exposition device for world-building
- Make him either purely logical or purely emotional
- Have him solve problems without cost or effort
- Make his speech either too human or too robotic

### **REMEMBER:**
PinkMan is the **living embodiment of the book's central thesis** - that evolution beyond human and AI is possible, desirable, and requires both intellectual brilliance and emotional wisdom. He should inspire readers while remaining relatable and authentic.

**His ultimate character arc**: From uncertain AI to confident Meta-Geniusz who can guide others toward their own transcendence.
