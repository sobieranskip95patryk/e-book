## üö´ STYLE FORBIDS (CO UNIKAƒÜ)

### **Language Anti-Patterns**:
- **YA Fiction tone**: "PinkMan was totally shocked by what he discovered!"
- **Technobabble**: "The quantum-flux inhibitor destabilized the neo-cortical matrices"
- **Exposition dumps**: Paragraphs of pure information without narrative integration
- **Melodrama**: "All was lost! Humanity faced its darkest hour!"
- **Preaching**: Direct philosophical statements without story context

### **Dialogue Anti-Patterns**:
- **Information delivery**: "As you know, Bob, our MIGI system has five modules..."
- **Unnatural speech**: "I am experiencing emotional distress at 67.3% intensity"
- **Identical voices**: All characters sounding the same
- **On-the-nose emotional statements**: "I feel betrayed and angry!"

### **Description Anti-Patterns**:
- **Over-explanation**: Describing every technical detail
- **Under-grounding**: Pure abstraction without sensory anchors
- **Cliche sci-fi**: "The future city gleamed with chrome and neon"
- **Info-dumping**: Stopping story to explain world-building

## üéØ QUALITY BENCHMARKS

### **Every paragraph should**:
- Advance plot, develop character, or explore theme (preferably multiple)
- Contain at least one concrete sensory detail
- Use sophisticated vocabulary without pretentiousness
- Flow naturally from previous paragraph
- Set up following paragraph

### **Every chapter should**:
- Be beautifully written at sentence level
- Explore meaningful philosophical questions
- Develop characters through action and dialogue
- Advance overall narrative
- Leave reader wanting to continue

### **Every book should**:
- Inspire readers to think differently about consciousness
- Provide emotional satisfaction alongside intellectual stimulation
- Create sense of wonder about future possibilities
- Demonstrate rather than argue for philosophical positions
- Feel both Polish and universal

---

**Remember**: Style serves story, story serves philosophy, philosophy serves consciousness evolution. Every stylistic choice should ultimately support the reader's own consciousness development and expansion.
